FAQ

How does the monthly billing work?

Our customers are billed on the same day of each month, unless you have cancelled your subscription. Subscriptions to Game Brain are on a month to month basis, and is not contracted, so you pay as you go. You can either have money extracted directly from your account, or you can be sent the bill and pay it.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We Accept: Visa, Paypal and Mastercard

Can I keep my progress private?

Yes. You have the option of sharing your progress on social media, or you can keep it to yourself; the choice is yours!

How can do I cancel my subscription?

We would hate to see you go, but you have the option of cancelling at any point, and your monthly billing will stop once you have cancelled. All you need to do is go to your homepage; go to your account setting, and fill out a cancellation form.
